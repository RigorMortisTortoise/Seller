Off-Site Sales & Night Cap Sales

Get the benefit of our award-winning auction technology, without delivering your inventory to a Copart facility. Fully integrated with our online auction technology, VB3, sellers can assign vehicles to off-site sales, save on shipping costs and have access to Copart's world-wide Buyer base.

Boats can be auctioned from any marina and heavy equipment can be auctioned where it sits. Night Cap sales give you more chances to sell your inventory by hosting auctions at times more convenient to international members and those that could not participate during our regular auction times.

 

Loan Payoff

Copart integrates with 162+ primary lenders and their affiliates to track real time loan payoff amounts through direct access to partner lender databases. With our loan payoff tool, there is no need to log in to dozens of different portals or access different companies reports to determine loan payoff amounts.

 
 

Title Procurement and Title Direct

Title Direct is a program that allows the insured and their lienholders to send title documentation directly to Copart, bypassing the seller. With Title Direct, Copart will handle your vehicle titling needs so your staff can focus on more important matters.

With Title Procurement Copart handles the title transfer process from beginning to end. We will process lien holder determination, obtaining letters of guarantee and notifying insurance partners when documents have been received. We have hundreds of dedicated title-procurement personnel across multiple locations throughout the U.S. Our titling services are designed to reduce costs while increasing efficiencies and returns.

Our systems can link seamlessly with other business' applications to ensure ease of access to your inventory being sold.

Text to Release Owner and Adjuster

Help increase cycle time with Text to Adjuster. Copart facilities can send texts directly to claims adjusters requesting vehicle status updates to help release units more quickly.

With the ability to text the insured as well, Copart handles post-accident communication to schedule pick up of vehicle and/or personal belongings that are often left in vehicles.

Dynamic Total Loss Express

Expedite the claim settlement process and increase efficiency with our Dynamic Total Loss Express service. You can eliminate the need for adjusters to travel for inspections, appraise more vehicles in less time and settle with customers more quickly. With the ability to deliver vehicles directly to our Copart locations for total loss appraisal, you can also avoid unnecessary towing and storage costs.

 
 

Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

Copart can ensure personal identifiable information like previous GPS coordinates, phone records, and other information is removed from vehicles before going through an auction.

After an accident, often the last thing on an owner's mind is clearing their personal data from their totaled vehicle. We want to ensure that personal data does not fall into the wrong hands. Copart can help ensure personal identifiable information like previously entered GPS coordinates, phone records, text messages, gate, and garage codes are removed from vehicles before going through an auction.

Proquote

ProQuote assists sellers in the vehicle claims evaluation process by providing online salvage estimates which help Sellers determine whether to repair a vehicle or deem it a total loss.

Our proprietary valuation tool delivers sale price estimates with pinpoint accuracy by leveraging millions of data points aggregated through our auctions – including make, model, year, severity of damage, season, scrap metal prices and proximity to ports.

Intelliseller

Copart's IntelliSeller makes data-driven decisions to ensure your inventory gets the highest return possible.

IntelliSeller uses machine learning based on data from millions of lots and historical sales data to determine precise minimum bid levels, counterbid amounts and sell decisions to get the most for your vehicles.
