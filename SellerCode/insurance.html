Titles

Title Direct is a program that allows the insured and their lienholders to send title documentation directly to Copart, bypassing the seller. With Title Direct, Copart will handle your vehicle titling needs so your staff can focus on more important matters.

Title Procurement – Let Copart handle the title transfer process from beginning to end. We will handle lien holder determination, obtaining letters of guarantee and notifying insurance partners when documents have been received. We have hundreds of dedicated title-procurement personnel across multiple locations throughout the U.S.
